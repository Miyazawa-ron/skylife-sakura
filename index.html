skyライフ co., ltd. redefines space — not as property, but as a place where people and communities connect. we breathe new life into existing buildings and overlooked spaces, turning them into homes for travelers, long-term residents, and the communities around them. design is at the heart of everything we do — not as decoration, but as a way of shaping how people live. our guiding principle: designing the region and the way we live.
